The Problem

Most NHS doctors don't know
what they're entitled to.

And it costs them. A lot.

💸

Missing OMP entirely

Missing one qualifying date or misunderstanding continuous service can drop you from £18,700 OMP to £10,000 SMP — a difference of nearly £8,000.

🔄

Rotational confusion

Changing trusts during training can feel like it resets everything. It doesn't have to — but you need to know the rules before HR gets it wrong.

📋

Information scattered everywhere

BMA documents, NHS Employers circulars, deanery FAQs — often contradictory, always exhausting to navigate while working full time and pregnant.
